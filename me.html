Privacy

Default visibility for new posts —

New Memories, Journal entries, and Journey events default to this visibility — change it from the Preferences tab.

  • Owner — full access everywhere, the only role that sees Connections/System Logs.
  • Friend — has their own private space for Memories/Journal/Finance/Journey/Habits.
  • Viewer — read-only, sees public content from the Owner and any Friend.
  • Finance is always private — it is never shown on anyone's public profile or Collection, regardless of role.
